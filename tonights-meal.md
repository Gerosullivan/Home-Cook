# Tonight's Meal — Wednesday 18 March 2026

## Pan-Fried Chicken with Butter Gravy & Potatoes

**Servings:** 5 | **Prep:** 15 mins | **Cook:** 45 mins | **Protein:** Chicken

---

### Ingredients

- 1 whole chicken, broken down into pieces
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- 30g butter (for gravy)
- 1 tbsp flour
- 300ml chicken stock
- 2 sprigs fresh thyme
- Half a lemon
- 750g baby potatoes
- 20g butter (for potatoes)
- 300g fine green beans

### Method

**1. Preheat & prep** *(2 mins)*
Preheat oven to 200°C. Break down the whole chicken into pieces: breasts on the bone with skin, legs, and thighs.

**2. Pan-fry the chicken** *(7 mins)*
Season chicken pieces with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a large oven-safe frying pan over medium-high heat. Place chicken pieces skin-side down and fry for 4–5 minutes until skin is golden and crispy. Turn and fry the other side for 2–3 minutes.

**3. Oven roast** *(20–25 mins)*
Transfer the pan to the oven. Cook until internal temperature reaches 68°C on the wireless thermometer — roughly 20–25 minutes depending on piece size. Remove chicken to a warm plate and rest.

> **Thermometer reminder:** Probe the thickest part of the breast and thigh. Target 68°C.

**4. Potatoes** *(15–18 mins — start when chicken goes in the oven)*
Boil baby potatoes in salted water for 15–18 minutes until tender. Drain, toss with 20g butter.

**5. Green beans** *(3–4 mins — start near the end of the potato cook)*
Boil or steam fine green beans for 3–4 minutes until tender-crisp. Drain.

**6. Butter gravy** *(5 mins — make while chicken rests)*
Return the frying pan to medium heat (careful — handle is hot!). Add 30g butter, let it foam. Stir in flour and cook for 1 minute. Pour in chicken stock gradually, stirring constantly. Add thyme sprigs and a squeeze of lemon. Simmer for 3–4 minutes until thickened into a glossy gravy. Season to taste.

**7. Serve**
Plate chicken pieces with potatoes, green beans, and gravy spooned over.
