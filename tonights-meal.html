🍝 Ger's Bolognese

~1 hr 50 mins total
Serves 5
Beef · Italian

Ingredients

  • 1 pack pancetta
  • 800g lean beef mince
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 3 sticks celery, diced
  • 3 medium onions, diced
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ¼ tube tomato purée
  • Splash of red wine vinegar
  • 400ml chicken stock (homemade)
  • 1 beef bouillon cube
  • 3 tins plum tomatoes
  • Splash of Worcestershire sauce
  • Splash of dark soy sauce
  • Smoked chipotle flakes, to taste
  • 1 Parmesan rind
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 500g pasta (rigatoni or pappardelle)
  • Parmesan, shredded, for serving
  • Fresh basil for serving

Method

1
Crisp the pancetta ~5 mins
Fry the pancetta in a wide stainless steel pan over medium-high heat until crispy. Remove and set aside.
2
Brown the mince ~10 mins
In the same pan, place 800g mince in golf-ball sized clumps. Let them sear underneath without moving — get a good crust. Then mash down and break apart until fully browned.
💡 Tip: Don't crowd the pan. If needed, brown in two batches for a better crust. This is where the deep flavour comes from.
3
Cook the soffritto ~10 mins
In a separate pan, heat a little olive oil. Add the diced onions, carrots, and celery with the rosemary, oregano, and salt. Cook until softened.
4
Caramelise and deglaze ~3 mins
Add ¼ tube of tomato purée to the veg. Let it cook on the bottom of the pan for a minute or two to caramelise. Add a good splash of red wine vinegar and stir through.
5
Combine and build the sauce ~5 mins
Combine the mince, pancetta, and softened veg in a large casserole pan. Mix 400ml chicken stock with beef bouillon until dissolved. Pour into the casserole.
6
Add the flavour bombs ~2 mins
Add 3 tins of plum tomatoes, a good splash of Worcestershire sauce, a good splash of dark soy sauce, smoked chipotle flakes to taste, and the Parmesan rind.
7
Simmer low and slow ~60+ mins
Bring to a simmer on the hob. Cook low and slow, stirring occasionally, until rich and thickened. At least 1 hour — longer is better. The sauce will simmer while you're on pickup.
8
Cook pasta and serve ~12 mins (after pickup)
Remove the Parmesan rind and rosemary stalks. Cook 500g pasta in plenty of salted boiling water. Drain and toss with the sauce. Serve with shredded Parmesan, torn fresh basil, and a drizzle of olive oil.
👧 Kid-friendly note: The chipotle is mild at 1 tsp — it adds smokiness, not heat. If the kids are particularly sensitive, hold the chipotle from their portions and stir it into the adults' bowls at the table. The Parmesan rind melts in and gives the sauce a lovely richness they'll love.
Leftovers: Bolognese freezes brilliantly. Portion into containers and freeze for up to 3 months. Reheat gently with a splash of water.
