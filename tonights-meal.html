🥘 Next Level Paella

~1 hr 10 mins total
Serves 5
Seafood · Spanish
Ger's version: Adapted from BBC Good Food. Frozen prawns (no shells), leftover roast chicken from Monday instead of mussels/squid, sherry vinegar instead of dry sherry, frozen fine beans instead of broad beans.

Ingredients

Scaled to 5 servings (recipe base is 4)

  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 375g frozen prawns, de-shelled and deveined
  • 375g leftover roast chicken, shredded
  • 1 bunch of parsley, stalks & leaves separated
  • 2½ tbsp sherry vinegar
  • Large pinch of saffron strands
  • 190g cooking chorizo, cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 310g paella rice
  • 125g frozen fine beans
  • 750ml chicken stock (or water)
  • 1 lemon, zested then cut into wedges
  • Smoked sea salt (optional)

Method

1
Make the saffron stock ~12 mins (before pickup)
Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wide, shallow pan. Add the parsley stalks and sizzle for a minute. Add 2½ tbsp sherry vinegar and 750ml chicken stock (or water). Add the saffron, season with salt and simmer for 10 minutes. Strain into a bowl — you need about 875ml total (scaled for 5), top up with water if needed. Let the saffron steep.
2
Chorizo, soffritto & rice ~10 mins (after pickup)
Wipe out the pan and add the rest of the olive oil. Sizzle the chorizo until it releases its oil, then add the onion and garlic and cook until softened. Add the tomatoes and cook for a few minutes until pulpy. Tip in the rice and stir until coated, then pour in the saffron stock. Bring to a simmer.
💡 Tip: Once the stock is in, resist stirring too much — you want a slight crust (socarrat) to form on the bottom. Stir gently a couple of times early on, then leave it alone.
3
Simmer the rice ~15 mins
Cook uncovered for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally at first then leaving it alone. The stock should be almost absorbed and the rice almost cooked.
4
Add prawns, beans & chicken ~5 mins
Tuck the defrosted prawns into the rice and simmer for 3–4 minutes until pink and cooked through. Stir through the frozen fine beans to heat through, and scatter the shredded leftover roast chicken over the top to warm.
5
Rest & serve
Once the rice is just cooked, turn off the heat and cover with a tea towel for a few minutes. Scatter over the parsley leaves, lemon zest, and smoked sea salt if using. Serve with lemon wedges.
👧 Kid-friendly note: This paella is mild — the chorizo adds warmth but no heat. Serve the kids' portions from the centre of the pan where the rice is softest. The leftover chicken makes it familiar for the little ones.
Leftovers: Paella reheats well — splash in a little water, cover with foil, and warm in the oven at 160°C for 15 mins.
